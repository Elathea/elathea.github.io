Code of Conduct

We encourage users of Descent Engine to foster an open and collaborative community. While not mandatory, you can promote this ethos by:

  • Exposing as much functionality of your work as possible to your users and avoiding obfuscation.
  • Providing documentation for interacting with and modifying your work.
  • Developing and supporting tools that enable the modification of your work, and not subverting such tools built by others.
  • Refraining from taking legal action against the creation and distribution of modifications to your work (as long as it does not involve the redistribution of your work itself or assets contained within).
